Church

Definition
The place where God lives.

Sign Description
Fingertips on both hands facing each other, bent at 90o, and fingers in each hand touching. Move in one circle. One hand going up and away from the body, the other going down and towards the body. Once a single circle has been drawn, the two hands move away from each other. 

You may also start the sign at point upwards toward the sky. Noting where heaven is.
